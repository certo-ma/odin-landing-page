The three metamorphoses of the Spirit
What is heavy?
so asks the spirit that would bear much,
and then kneels down like the camel,
and wants to be well laden.
"Thou-shalt," is the great dragon called.
But the spirit of the lion says, "I will".
He will seize his freedom
and be master in his own wilderness.
The child is innocence and forgetting,
a new beginning, a game, a self-rolling wheel,
a first movement, a sacred Yes.
For the game of creation, my brothers,
a sacred Yes is needed.
"I am a forest, and a night of dark trees: but he who is not afraid of my darkness, will find banks full of roses under my cypresses".
- Friedrich Nietzsche
